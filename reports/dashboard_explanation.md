# 📊 Power BI Dashboard Explanation

## 📌 Overview
A 4-page interactive dashboard was created to monitor supply chain performance.

---

## 🟦 Page 1: Executive Overview
- Total Shipments
- Disruption Rate
- Average Lead Time
- Risk Score
- Reliability Score
- Trend over time

---

## 🟥 Page 2: Risk Analysis
- Disruption by risk level
- Weather impact analysis
- High-risk origin ports
- Shipment-level risk details

---

## 🟨 Page 3: Transport & Operations
- Lead time by transport mode
- Reliability comparison
- Efficiency metrics
- Monthly shipment trends

---

## 🟩 Page 4: Product & External Factors
- Disruption by product category
- Weather distribution
- Fuel price vs lead time
- External risk indicators

---

## 🎯 Business Value
- Helps identify high-risk shipments
- Supports route optimization
- Enables proactive risk management
