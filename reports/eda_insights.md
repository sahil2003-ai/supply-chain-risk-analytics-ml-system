# 📊 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 🔍 Disruption Analysis
- Disruption rates are higher in high-risk regions
- Medium and high-risk levels show significantly more disruptions

---

## 🌦️ Weather Impact
- Hurricanes and storms show highest disruption rates
- Clear weather has lowest impact

---

## 🚢 Transport Mode Analysis
- Air transport has lowest lead time but higher variability
- Sea transport shows longer delays

---

## 📦 Product Category
- Disruption rate is similar across categories
- Slight variation observed in textiles and pharmaceuticals

---

## 📈 Lead Time Analysis
- Lead time distribution is right-skewed
- Higher lead time increases disruption probability

---

## 🔥 Correlation Insights
- Lead time and risk score positively correlate with disruptions
- Carrier reliability negatively correlates with disruption
